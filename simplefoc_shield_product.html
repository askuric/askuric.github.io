SimpleFOCShield

Low cost modular BLDC motor driver board

The aim of this Arduino Shield is to provide an low-cost, modular and "user-friendlier" alternative to the BLDC motors with FOC algorithm.
It is intended primarily for beginners which want to understand how this algorithm works and understand its benefits and constraints.
For advanced users, this board will be a good testing setup where they can optimise the algorithm for their application.

  • Plug & play

    Fully compatible with SimpleFOC library and most of the Arduino boards

  • Stackable

    Possibility of stacking 2 shields (2 BLDC motors + 2 position sensors)

  • Configurable pinout

    Possible to choose all the pins used by the board.

  • Encoder Pull-Ups

    Integrated configurable 3KOhm Encoder pull-ups

  • Open-Source

    Schematics and fabrication files fully available.
